Pesto pasta

Cook this delicious student staple

Cooking time: 30 minutes
Difficulty rating: ⭐

Add your favourite ingredients to this dish and it's the perfect dish to use up some of your leftovers!







Ingredients

75g of dried pasta
1 tbsp of pesto
A handful of vegetables - anything from tomatoes to cucumber
Looking to add some meat? Why not add some pre-cooked barbecque chicken?


Method

  1. Boil some water and cook your pasta until it's aldente (with a bit of bite)
  2. Drain off the water, and mix together your ingredients
  3. Enjoy!
