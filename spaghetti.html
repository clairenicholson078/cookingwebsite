Spaghetti Bolognese

Impress your mates with this Italian classic!

Cooking time: 30 minutes
Difficulty rating: ⭐

Ingredients

75g dried pasta per person
A tin of chopped tomatoes
A teaspon of dried basil
Beef mince
Cheddar cheese
Additions to taste such as mushrooms


Method

  1. Boil your pasta until it's aldente
  2. Brown off your mince until it's no longer pink
  3. Add your chopped tomatoes
  4. Add your additions - don't add them too early on or they'll burn!
